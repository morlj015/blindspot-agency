Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Blindspot uses personal information submitted through this website. Last updated: 20 March 2026.

Who we are

Blindspot is a marketing agency. If you have any privacy questions, email harry@blindspot.agency.

What we collect

  • Contact details you give us, such as your name, email address and business name.
  • Information you provide in enquiry forms, booking requests and quote requests.
  • Technical preference information stored on your device, such as theme selection and cookie notice acknowledgement.

Why we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry or quote request.
  • To take steps before entering into a contract with you.
  • To run and secure the website.
  • To remember site preferences you actively choose.

Legal bases

We generally process enquiry and lead information because it is necessary to respond to your request or because we have a legitimate interest in handling business enquiries and operating this site.

Sharing

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the site or handle enquiries, such as form processing tools and hosting providers. We do not sell personal information submitted through this website.

Retention

We keep enquiry information only for as long as reasonably necessary for follow-up, record keeping, legal obligations and business administration.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to our use of your personal data, and to complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office.

More information

Read our Cookie Policy and Terms of Use. If your business needs a more detailed processing schedule, contact us before submitting sensitive information.
